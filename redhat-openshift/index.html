Overview

The way software and applications are delivered to users and companies has changed a lot in recent years. Although SaaS now has become the go-to model to serve modern applications, often there comes a scenario when it does not serve the purpose. Enterprises especially with their stricter procurement policies and processes, demand that they host a service the traditional way, on-premise, instead of accessing it over the web or cloud for reasons such as: a higher level of security, more guaranteed uptime, robustness and flexibility.

To cater to demands as such, we are glad to announce that APIMatic can now be deployed on-premise, on your hardware, virtual machines, or on private clouds. To deliver this we have partnered up with Red Hat to serve our platform via their OpenShift Container Platform.

Red HatOpenShiftis a platform for standardized, self-service dev environments and full-stack automated ops, so you can stay focused on the code that will make a difference. It offers automated installation, upgrades, and lifecycle management throughout the container stackthe operating system, Kubernetes and cluster services, and applicationson any cloud.

Now you can easily deploy services such as theAPIMatic CodeGenandAPIMatic Transformeron your local machines and favourite environments, while enjoying the maximum support from our team.
